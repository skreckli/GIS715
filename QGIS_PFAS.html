GLobal PFAS Study Locations Extracted from Literature

Human and Surface Water PFAS Studies in Asia

Locations of global PFAS studies were extracted from peer-reviewed literature and georeferenced. In QGIS, a neon effect was applied to the points to make the locations stand out from the dark grey background. The points were colored light blue for surface water and bright pink for human study locations. The neon effect was achieved by enabling an outer glow draw effect using a gradient color ramp which ranged from the point color to the grey background color. A second point in a lighter color saturation was added to enhance the neon effect. The point layers were applied an 85% opacity to increase the visibility of points behind other points.
